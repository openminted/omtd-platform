License Compatibility Matrix

The OpenMinTeD License Compatibility Matrix and Tool aim to help users understand whether they can combine together works, software and services available under different licenses or terms of use and determine what they can do with the outcome of their combination. By selecting two licences you will otain a result in the form of: YES, YES (under conditions) and NO. Under "more details" you have access to the legal analysis employed for the compatibility calculation.

However, it should be borne in mind that the combination of resources for TDM does not alway lead to the creation of a derivative work (which is normally the condition for a licence, especially an OA/FLOSS licence, to be persistent) and in these cases there should be no restriction under copyright law to the possibility to combine them, (but you should ensure that your use of the resources is lawful, which is normally the case if you use Open Access/FLOSS licences or your use is covered by an exception or limitation to copyright). There are many cases where the difference is not clear cut and the specificities of applicable law, of the specific type of TDM alghorithms employed as well as the specific terminology employed by the licenses can become decisive. For an example of when training a ML modle for NPL purposes creates a derivative work and when it does not, OpenMinTeD has produced this study which you may find useful (but it is a research study not legal advice, it is for research and general information purposes only): Link1 and Link2
