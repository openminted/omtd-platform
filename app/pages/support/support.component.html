Technical

OpenMinTeD GitHub technical issues

The OpenMinTeD platform is now at the operational state but bugs or questions may come up while using it. You can check for answers or submit your questions at the OpenMinTeD GitHub for technical discussions ( OpenMinTeD github issues ). Discuss your issues with fellow users and experts, receive answers and help improve the platform.

Interoperability Guidelines

If you are looking to contribute with content or TDM tools, services and auxiliary resources to OpenMinTeD, you should check our guidelines containing instructions on how to create metadata and how to register your content and TDM resources to OpenMinTeD.

Legal

FAQ Legal

Is the complex legal landscape holding you back from using our platform and doing TDM? Don't worry, you are not the only one. We compiled the most frequently asked legal questions and answers to help you out and get you on your way again.

License Compatibility Matrix

Do you want to mine a set of texts that are distributed with different licenses? Or build a new TDM workflow with components licensed with different licenses or terms of use? Would you like to see what you are allowed to do with these combinations and their outcomes? Our license compatibility matrix can help you find out!

Training and resources on text and data mining

If you are looking for general information on text and data mining on educational resources, try our OpenMinTeD knowledge base on the Foster platform. We collected resources and are working on online courses on TDM.
